About Institution

Government Girls Polytechnic is a government institute run by government of Uttar Pradesh Govt girls polytechnic Varanasi is established in academic year 1990-91 Inauguration of main building was held by honourable Dr Narendra Kumar Singh Gour, Minister, Technical Education UP and in chairmanship of honourable shri Shankar Prasad jawal member of parliament Varanasi on 31st January 1999. This institute is approved by AICTE, New Delhi & affiliated to Board of technical education UP Lucknow it as only institute for girls in Varanusi Gove Girls Polytechnic a committed to provide quality education to the students & enabling them to excel in the field of engineering to cater to the changing & challenging needs of society and industry by contributing to the academic standing and overall knowledge development of the students Maintaining a state of software infrastructure and congenial learning environment The disanctive mission of the institute to serve society as a center of higher learning providing long term societal benefits through transmitting advanced knowledge, in the field of Information Technology Fashion Technology Textile Designing and Electronics Engineering Technical education plays an important role in the socio-economic development of the country in general emancipation,.

Our Vision

The Govt. Girls Polytechnic Varanasi strives to be among leading educational institutions of the Country by consistently engaging in its endeavor through quality teaching and innovation rooted in the Indian Value System, and working towards building an intellectual infrastructure that serves the Country, its society and humanity.

Our Mission

To deliver student centric research-based education with emphasis on innovation to unlock opportunities by building trusted partnerships with stakeholders, to integrate policy with practice and to enable young minds to embark upon the journey of intellectual, social and spiritual transformation. .
